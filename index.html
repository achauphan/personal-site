At MSI, I help provide support and guidance to our University research staff using our high-performance computing equipment. This includes helping researchers write & optimize data intensive computational scripts, guidance on writing resource efficient job submissions to our Slurm Workload scheduler, and other questions concerning the usage of our available software modules. Additionally, I help users with other non-technical inquiries such as connecting researchers to MSI staff members for specific consultations, group & user account difficulties, & other general questions & requests.

Currently, I am pursuing internship opportunities to gain hands on experience within software development & system administration.
